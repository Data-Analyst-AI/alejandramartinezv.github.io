Projects

Here are some of my 14 recent projects, each designed to solve real business challenges through data. Click on the image to explore the full analysis and recommendations.

Ice Games Project: Video Game Sales Analysis

Simulation of predictive analysis on historical video game sales in the Ice online store. Identified patterns of commercial success, impact of reviews, and regional trends. Includes hypothesis testing and recommendations for marketing campaigns in 2017.

Chicago Trip Data: Urban Mobility Analysis

Study of taxi travel patterns in Chicago during November 2017. Identified most visited neighborhoods, performance of transportation companies, and conducted a hypothesis test on the impact of weather on trip duration. Tools: Python, pandas, seaborn, SciPy.

Food App Behavior: Conversion Analysis and A/A/B Experiment

Exploration of user behavior in a food app. Identified critical points in the conversion funnel and evaluated the impact of a typographic redesign through an A/A/B experiment. The analysis revealed that the visual change did not generate significant differences. Tools: Python, pandas, matplotlib, scipy.

Marketing Optimization: ROI and Budget Reallocation

Analysis of over two years of data on the Showz platform to identify the most profitable acquisition channels. Key metrics (LTV, CAC, ROMI) were calculated, device segmentation was applied, and efficient reallocation of the marketing budget was recommended. Tools: cohorts, heat maps, A/B testing.

Retention Strategy: Churn Prediction in Model Fitness

Project focused on detecting silent churn of customers in the fitness industry. A churn predictive model was built, profiles were segmented using clustering, and personalized strategies were formulated to improve retention. Tools: Python, scikit-learn, K-Means, Random Forest.
